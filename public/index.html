Innovative Engineering Solutions For Chemical Industries

Novatech Professional Corp. is a chemical engineering & consulting firm providing services to chemical industries. Industries served have included the nitrogen industries (including energetic materials), the fuel ethanol industry, and other acid industries (including metal plating & acid recovery). Novatech is licensed with AspenPLUS to provide process simulation.

Novatech also provides project management to help pull a project together, champion the project through full coordination with clients' staff, and move the project through to completion and commissioning.

Civil, structural, electrical engineering (certified drawings approved for construction)

Commissioning; Project management; and so forth
